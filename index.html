    ACT 1:

    • Wapira's Crown: You get Wapira's Crown as a reward from Zevlor for doing the Save the Refugees quest, provided you accept the monetary reward. (Alternatives Circlet of Blasting, Haste Helm, or Grymskull Helm).
    • Luminous Armour: Chest in Selunite Outpost, Underdark (X:176 Y:-247). (Alternatives Adamantine Scale Mail, or Githyanki Half Plate).
    • Gloves of Missile Snaring: Sold from Arron in Druid Grove Act 1. (Alternative Hellrider's Pride).
    • Disintegrating Night Walkers: Obtained in Gyrmforge as part of Free True Soul Nere questline (Alternatives Boots of Striding, Boots of Genial Striding, or Boots of Speed).
    • Pearl of Power Amulet: It is sold by Omeluum in the Myconid Colony, in the Underdark. To trade with them, you must first finish their quest to help them investigate the parasite.
    • Ring of Absolute Force: Dropped by Sergeant Thrinn in Grymforge. (Alternative Ring of Colour Spray).
    • The Sparkswall: Obtained in a chest in Arcane Tower, Underdark Act 1. (Alternative Crusher Ring).
    • Adamantine Shield: Crafted in Gyrmforge, Underdark Act 1. (Alternative Glowing Shield, or Wood Woad Shield).
    • The Blood of Lathander: Legendary mace with a +3 bonus to its Attack Rolls and Damage ACT 1 in the colostor.

    ACT 2:

    • Shield of Devotion: Sold by Quartermaster Talli in Last Light Inn, in Act 2.
    • The Whispering Promise: Purchase from Roah Moonglow in either Shattered Sanctum (Act 1) or Moonrise Towers (Act 2).
    • Spellcrux Amulet: Drop by The Warden in Moonrise Towers Prison (X:569 Y:-650) (Alternative: Amulet of the Devout or Amulet of Greater Health).

    ACT 3:

    • Hood of the Weave: It is sold by Mystic Carrion in Philgrave's Mansion in Act 3.
    • Armour of Landfall: t gives another +1 buff to your Spell Save DC. (Alternative: Armour of Agility)
      - If Rolan is dead - Sold by Lorroakan's Projection in Sorcerous Sundries
      - If Rolan and Lorroakan are both alive - Sold by Rolan in Sorcerous Sundries
      - If Rolan is alive but Lorroakan is dead - Pickpocket from Rolan in Ramazith's Tower
    • Helldusk Gloves: +1 bonus to Spell Save DC and Spell Attack Rolls. They are dropped by Haarlep in the House of Hope. (Alternative: Quickspell Gloves).
    • Helldusk Boots: Gilded Chest on the top floor of Wyrm's Rock Fortress. Take the boots before Gortash's Coronation to avoid making nearby NPCs hostile. (Alternative: Disintegrating Night Walkers).
    • Ring of Feywild Sparks: This ring has a hidden +1 bonus to Spell Save DC that's not included in the tooltip. Looted from Auntie Ethel during Act 3. You will need to kill the Hag in her new lair underneath the Blushing Mermaid to get this ring. (Alternative: Ring of Free Action or Killer's sweetheart).
    • Crypt Lord Ring: The Find Mystic Carrion's Servant quest reward, Act 3.
    • Markoheshkir: At Ramazith's Tower (Alternative Staff of Cherished Necromancy, Staff of Spellpower, or Incandescent Staff).
    • Devotee's Mace: This Mace has a +3 bonus to Attack Rolls and Damage, can be obtained at Level 10 by using your Divine Intervention Action and choosing Arm Thy Servant.
    • Viconia's Walking Fortress: This shield is looted from Viconia DeVir in the Cloister of Sombre Embrace, in Act 3.
    • The Dead Shot: It is sold by Fytz the Firecracker in Stormshore Armoury, Lower City.
